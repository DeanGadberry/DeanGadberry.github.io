Healthy Eating for Gut and Brain

What you eat directly impacts your gut microbiome, neurotransmitter production, and brain function. Focus on nutrient-rich foods that nourish both your gut and your mind:

  • Fiber-rich vegetables and fruits: Support gut flora and aid in producing key neurotransmitters.
  • Polyphenol-rich foods: Berries, dark chocolate, and green tea help boost BDNF and brain health.
  • Fermented foods: Yogurt, kefir, kimchi, and sauerkraut introduce beneficial bacteria to the gut.
  • Healthy fats: Omega-3 sources like salmon, chia seeds, and walnuts improve cognitive function and reduce inflammation.
  • Whole grains and legumes: Provide prebiotic fibers that feed good gut bacteria.
  • Limit processed sugar and artificial additives: These can disrupt gut balance and increase neuroinflammation.
